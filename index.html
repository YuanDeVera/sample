Noli Me Tangere Characters

Crisostomo Ibarra
    Crisostomo Ibarra studied in europe for his education. He believes that with his good education he can save Filipinos.
Maria Clara
    Maria Clara was raised as the daughter of Capitan Tiago. She is childhood friends with Crisostomo Ibarra. Maria Clara loved Ibarra as much as she loved her family and ultimately decided to choose her family and left her guilty.
Padre Damaso
    Padre Damaso was a priest in the Philippines serving three years in a small town. He became close friends with Captain Tiago.He is arrogant and sometimes feels superiour than others.
Elias
    Elias was born in a rich family. Elias was a selfless man and had a code with honor. He grew up wealthy. After losing everything he had and learning the true history of his family.
Rafael Ibarra
    He is Chrisostomo's late father and he was falsely accused of making a crime and got sent to prison on where he would soon meet his death.
